About Tinomudaishe Ndhlovu

I am a self taught game and android developer. I am currently leading a group of enthusiastic software engineers at an educational technology company where we connect private organizations to schools and other civic organizations as well as digitize the workflow processes in various industries. I also work part-time with another technology company that builds games and android applications for educating the cooperate world in fun and easy ways.

I am part of the Pycon(Python Conference) Namibia organizing committee(Django Team), where we help grow the industry by hosting workshops on topics related to python programming and fields affected by it such as Django, Data Analytics etc. I am also a part of the Namibian Women's Association(The Children's Movement) that focuses on educating young people from the ages of 8 to 28 on becoming self sustainable. I am currently pursuing several online certifications from the leading universities and leading industry expects on topics such as the z/oS Mainframe development from IBM, Back-End Developer from Meta, Blockchain Specialist from the University of Buffalo and NYU, Google Project Management Certification. I have a deep interest in consistently evolving in my skills set and education.
